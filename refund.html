General Policy

All stainless works fabricated are custom-built based on customer measurement, therefore cannot be reused or resold elsewhere. Refunds only apply before cutting, welding or material usage begins.

1. Cancellation Before Work Begins

  • Refund request must be made within 24 hours of payment.
  • 15% administrative deduction applies.
  • If no materials have been purchased, refund is immediate.

2. Work Already Started

  • Cost of cut stainless materials will be deducted.
  • If welding has begun, refund only applies to remaining labour portion.
  • Fabricated parts cannot be refunded.

3. Installation Stage

  • No refund once installation has started on-site.
  • Customer-driven changes requiring rework are billable.
  • Returned items after installation removed attract labour fee.
