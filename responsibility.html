Customer Responsibilities Before & During Service

To ensure smooth service and timely delivery, customers are expected to meet the obligations below before and during installation or fabrication.

1. Site Preparation

  • Grant access on agreed working dates.
  • Tiling and flooring must be fully completed.
  • Provide electricity access for welding and drilling.

2. Measurements & Approval

  • Final measurements must be confirmed before fabrication.
  • Any structural changes after measurement affect costing.

3. Payment Schedule

  • Deposit payment confirms project commencement.
  • Balance is paid immediately after installation.

4. Work Access

  • Allow sufficient space for movement of tools and technicians.
  • Ensure no interference or obstruction during process.

5. Post Installation

  • Inspect installation before technicians exit site.
  • Changes after team leaves attract service charges.
