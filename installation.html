Before Installation

Kindly ensure these conditions are met for proper installation, clean finishing and measurement accuracy.

1. Site Readiness

  • Floor/tiling must be completed.
  • Stair edges must be plastered and levelled.
  • Site should be dry and free from water leaks.
  • Electricity must be available for tools.

2. Measurement Requirements

  • Measurements are taken after finishing works.
  • Changes made afterwards affect price.
  • Final measurement approval is needed before fabrication.

3. Safety Requirements

  • Ladder or scaffold must be available for upstairs work.
  • Work paths must be accessible.
  • Keep children and unauthorized persons away.

4. After Installation

  • Customer must inspect before team exits.
  • Polishing and cleanup are included on same day.
  • Corrections after departure attract service fee.
