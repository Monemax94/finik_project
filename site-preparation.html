Readiness Checklist

Ensure all conditions below are ready to avoid delays, extra charges or rescheduling.

  • Final tiles and floors fully installed
  • Stair edges plastered and aligned
  • Wall finishing nearly completed
  • Electricity supply available for drilling/welding
  • Work area dry and free of leaks
  • Debris and obstruction cleared from work path

Access & Safety Setup

  • Gate opened and access path cleared
  • Material drop space prepared
  • Stairway free from stored materials
  • Ladder/scaffolding provided for upstairs work
  • Children & pets kept away from work zone

After Installation

  • Inspect work while team is still onsite
  • Technicians polish and clean stainless surfaces
  • Changes after team leaves attract service charges
