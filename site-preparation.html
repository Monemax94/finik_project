Readiness Checklist

Ensure these are ready before installation begins to avoid delays, rescheduling or extra charges.

  • Final tiles and floors fully installed
  • Stair edges plastered and aligned
  • Wall finishing almost completed
  • Electricity supply available (for drilling/welding)
  • Work environment dry and leak-free
  • Space cleared of debris and obstruction

Access & Safety Setup

  • Gate opened and access path cleared
  • Space provided for material dropping
  • Steps free from stored materials
  • Ladder/scaffolding available for upstairs floors
  • Children/pets kept away from work zone

After Installation

  • You inspect work while team is still on-site
  • Technicians polish and wipe stainless surfaces
  • Changes after team leaves attract service fees
