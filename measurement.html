Measurement Policy Overview

Accurate on-site measurements determine fabrication cutting sizes, fitting alignment, material cost and installation plan. Measurements are done after visible finishing work.

1. Site Measurement Requirements

  • Floor tiles must be fully installed and leveled.
  • Stair edges must be straight and plastered.
  • Main wall finishing must be completed.
  • Measurement should not be taken during ongoing structural changes.

2. On-site Measurement Charges

3. Cost Determination Factors

  • Actual stainless length measured (meter basis).
  • Height, turning angles, joints and bends.
  • Accessories used â€” rods, bars, clamps, holders, corners etc.
  • Steel grade: 304 or 316 stainless.
  • Complexity of installation location.

4. When Fresh Measurement is Required

  • Height of tiles or edges changed.
  • Walls rebuilt or extended after initial measurement.
  • Stairs reshaped or recast.
  • New dimensions differ from original record.

Any change after fabrication begins leads to price adjustments.

5. Final Measurement Confirmation

  • Customer must confirm final sizes verbally or via invoice.
  • Payment is based on validated measurement.
  • Fabrication begins only after appropriate deposit is received.
